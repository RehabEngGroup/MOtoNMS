Appendix A: Setup Files
-----------------------

This appendix lists the setup files already available in MOtoNMS distribution. They are stored in  ``MOtoNMS\SetupFiles\`` folder (:ref:`Code organization figure <codeorg>`). They are selected by the users through the GUIs to create the configuration files (``acquisition.xml``, ``elaboration.xml`` and ``static.xml``) required for processing.
